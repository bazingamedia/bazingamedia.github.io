About

Our journey.

  • 2015-2016

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We were trying to understand the digital marketing space by playing around with the different traffic sources, ad formats, offers and niches. We worked in everything from native, social, pops, video and banners.

  • August 2016-July2018

    eCommerce: Around the world

    We mastered Facebook Ads and ran massively successful campaigns, scaling products to more than 7 figures and building multiple stores. We sold to customers all around the world.

  • August 2018

    Time to Make (It) In India

    We decided our true love is India and what better way than to focus on our home market. So we started exploring the eCommerce space here. India was a different market, where we controlled the entire chain, from sourcing to packaging to shipping to customer support to marketing. We learnt a lot of things in the process.

  • January 2019

    Expansion

    It was time to scale up our operations in India, we took a bigger warehouse, hired a bigger operations team and opened up our doors to other people so that they can leverage our expertise and do what we did, without having to put in all the hard work.
